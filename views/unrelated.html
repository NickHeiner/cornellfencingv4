Welcome to the Home Energy Report program!
 
At UtilityCo, we are committed to helping our customers save on their energy bills.

Each month we will send you an email showing how your electricity use compares to your neighbors. This will help you understand whether your home uses a large or small amount of electricity.
 
 
Here's what your neighbor comparison might look like:
 
 
How is your home compared to your neighbors'?
 
Your electricity use is compared to approximately 100 nearby, occupied homes with similar characteristics, such as square footage and heating type.

To ensure your comparison depends only on things you can control, like replacing incandescent bulbs with LEDs, we chose the nearby homes most similar to yours.
